## ğŸ“Š Data Insights

### Key Findings:

**1. Claim Settlement Performance**
- Settled claims dominate at 4.4K cases, indicating strong processing efficiency
- Pending claims (2.3K) suggest ongoing review and processing activities
- Rejected claims (3.4K) show need for process improvement in application validation

**2. Revenue Distribution**
- Travel insurance generates highest premium revenue (2.5M)
- Health insurance is second-largest segment (1.2M)
- Opportunity to expand Home insurance segment (currently 0.6M)

**3. Age Group Insights**
- Adults contribute 8.8M in claim amounts (52% of total)
- Young adults are significant contributors (6.4M - 38% of total)
- Elder segment (1.7M - 10% of total) has lower claim amounts despite higher risk

**4. Policy Health**
- 58% active policy retention rate indicates good customer stickiness
- 42% inactive policies represent potential for re-engagement campaigns
- Balanced gender distribution ensures diverse customer base

**5. Claim Status by Policy Type**
- Travel policies have highest pending and rejected claims
- Auto policies show strong settlement rates
- Health policies require focus on pending claim resolution
